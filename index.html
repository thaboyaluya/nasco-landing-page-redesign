Welcome to NASCO Group

NASCO Group is one of West Africa’s leading Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) companies. From our headquarters in Nigeria, we manufacture and distribute a diverse range of trusted food and household brands.

We take pride in delivering superior products with uncompromising quality, innovation, and value for money. Millions of consumers rely on NASCO to bring excellence into their everyday lives.
